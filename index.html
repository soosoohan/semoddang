✨ TRINKLE ✨

Trinkle = Triangle + Twinkle

🎮 Game Rules
• Two players take turns drawing lines
• Complete a triangle to score and take another turn
• The player who draws more triangles wins
• When it's your turn, draw a line
• To play together, enter both names below and press '2 Player Mode'
• If playing alone, you'll compete against Trinky
• When playing against Sleepy Trinky, the player has a slight advantage
• To play against Trinky, press the 'Play vs Trinky' button
